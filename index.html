Who am I?

My name is Ken Vandivort. I grew up in Tulsa, Oklahoma and went to Oklahoma State University. I moved to Austin in 2005 and have dedicated the past ten years of work to helping people help themselves get clean and sober. I started to learn web design online at home on FreeCodeCamp in 2016. Then I started attending Austin Coding Academy in 2017 to take my knowledge to a professional level. I am excited to see what the future holds in my new web designer life. I enjoy good dark coffee, loud music, and big dogs.

Technical Skills

User Experience design and theory, wireframing, Adobe XD, Sketch, Git/Github, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Flexbox, Javascript, JQuery.

Human Skills

I am known to be empathetic and calm. Patience is one of my best virtues and tested often. Also, I'm certified in non-violent crisis intervention.

Extra Skills

Aquired extranious music knowledge. I've played the drums since I was 13 years old, but not so much these days. I play well with dogs and humans.
